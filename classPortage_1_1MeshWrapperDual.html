Detailed Description

template<class Mesh_Wrapper_Type>
class Portage::MeshWrapperDual< Mesh_Wrapper_Type >

Wrapper for dual mesh.

Utilizes a Mesh_Wrapper to the original mesh, but treats the nodes of the original mesh as the centroids of the dual mesh.
